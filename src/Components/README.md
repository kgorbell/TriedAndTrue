
# ***  COMPONENT LIBRARY  ***

Contains all components built with the intention to be re-used and consumed for various purposes. These components have been separated into the following three categories based upon their capabilities and purpose.


# Fragments

The most basic building block for building the UI. They are largely concerned with their own state, and will primarily provide consistency in styling across the site.


# Elements

Relatively complex components with the intention of performing/managing one or two tasks. May or may not consume Fragments in order to meet their requirements.


# Models

For our purposes, these are the most complex components available for re-use. They will handle multiple tasks/state management functions for the Page that consumes it.
